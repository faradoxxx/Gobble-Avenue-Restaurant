About Us

We Leave A Delicious Memory For You

Gobble Avenue is a delightful 1950s-themed diner located in Santa Rosa Laguna, known for its delectable selection of pasta, pizza, milkshakes, and grilled dishes. The diner's retro ambiance is immediately evident from the moment you step in, with vibrant neon lights, checkerboard flooring, and vintage posters adorning the walls. Gobble Avenue Youtube Channel

Welcome To Diner's Society

Quality of Food and Service
Best Quality Product

At Gobble Avenue, quality is of the utmost importance. The diner is committed to serving only the best, freshest ingredients to ensure that every dish is bursting with flavor.

Friendly Service

The staff at Gobble Avenue is friendly and attentive, providing excellent service to ensure a memorable dining experience. The restaurant is a popular spot for families, friends, and couples to gather and enjoy delicious food in a fun and nostalgic setting.

Responsive to a Feedback

Gobble Avenue has a number of ways that customers can provide feedback, including through social media, email, and in-person comments. The restaurant's staff makes a conscious effort to listen to feedback and take action where necessary.
